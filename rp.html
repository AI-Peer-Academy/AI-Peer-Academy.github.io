About Us

Welcome to **AI-Peer-Academy by MKM**, where we help aspiring researchers, including beginners, publish high-quality **review papers**.  Last year, we successfully published a **Generative AI-based research paper** in a **Q4 Scopus journal**, and we are excited to continue this journey.

Our goal for 2025 is to publish **12 review papers**—one every month. The first paper for this year is focused on **Data Science, Generative AI, and Computer Vision** and will be published this month.

Each paper can have a maximum of **6 members**. Currently, **2 slots are filled**. As a beginner, you are welcome to join us as a co-author, even if it's your first step into research paper publishing!

Why Join Us?

  • Beginner-friendly guidance for research paper preparation and publishing.
  • Opportunities to work on cutting-edge topics like **Generative AI**, **Data Science**, and **Computer Vision**.
  • Expert reviews, freelancer support (if needed), and full publishing cost coverage.
  • Targeted publishing in Scopus **Q3 or Q4 journals**.
  • Comprehensive peer-learning sessions to grow as a researcher.

This cost includes everything: guidance, expert reviews, development charges (if applicable), freelancer fees, publishing costs, and a donation for LMS (Learning Management System).
